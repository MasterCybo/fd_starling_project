An AIR mobile app written in ActionScript 3 on Starling Framework

Using libraries:
	Starling 1.6 - from 16.04.2015 http://gamua.com/starling/download/
	Feathers 2.1.1 - from 16.04.2015 http://feathersui.com/download/
	TweenMax 12.1.5 - from 16.04.2015 http://greensock.com/gsap-as
	StarlingFullScreenExtension - from 16.04.2015 https://github.com/Brinkbit/StarlingFullScreenExtension
	Starling-Extension-Graphics - from 16.04.2015 https://github.com/StarlingGraphics/Starling-Extension-Graphics
	SoundAS - from 16.04.2015 https://github.com/treefortress/SoundAS
	Gestouch - from 16.04.2015 https://github.com/fljot/Gestouch
